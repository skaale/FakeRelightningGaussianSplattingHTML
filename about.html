About

I'm a Technical Artist specializing in VR development and real-time graphics, based in Copenhagen. My work focuses on bridging the gap between artistic vision and technical implementation, developing tools and pipelines that empower creative teams to build immersive virtual reality experiences.

With a deep passion for real-time rendering and interactive graphics, I explore cutting-edge technologies like 3D Gaussian Splatting, neural rendering, and novel approaches to dynamic lighting. I believe in sharing knowledge openly and contributing tools that help the broader community push the boundaries of what's possible in real-time graphics.

I've had the opportunity to work on diverse VR projects spanning training simulations, therapeutic applications, architectural visualization, and interactive storytelling—each presenting unique technical challenges that drive innovation.

Current Focus

3D Gaussian Splatting

Developing Unity-based tools for working with 3D Gaussian Splats—a revolutionary representation for photorealistic scene capture. My research includes editor plugins for splat manipulation and novel approaches to real-time relighting of splat-based scenes.

VR Development

Creating immersive VR experiences with focus on performance optimization, visual fidelity, and user comfort. Working across the full stack from shader development to interaction design.

Technical Art & Tools

Building custom editor tools, shaders, and pipelines that accelerate production workflows. Passionate about democratizing complex graphics techniques through accessible tooling.
